Eco Activities

Earn points by completing these eco-friendly activities in your daily life!

Recycling Waste

50 Points/kg

Sort and recycle your household waste including plastic, paper, and glass.

Using Public Transport

20 Points/trip

Take the bus, train, or subway instead of driving your car.

Composting Food Scraps

100 Points/month

Start a compost pile for organic waste instead of throwing it away.

Reducing Energy Use

30 Points/kWh saved

Turn off lights and unplug electronics when not in use.

Planting Trees

200 Points/tree

Plant native trees in your yard or community spaces.

Using Reusable Bags

10 Points/use

Bring your own bags when shopping instead of using plastic ones.
