Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rewards program

How do I earn points?

You can earn points by completing eco-friendly activities listed on our Eco Activities page, such as recycling, using public transport, or reducing energy consumption.

Are points transferable?

No, points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder who earned them.

How long do points last?

Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned. Unused points will expire after this period.

Can I return a redeemed reward?

Rewards can be returned within 30 days if unused and in original condition. Points will be refunded minus a 50-point processing fee.

How do I track my progress?

Visit the Track Progress page to see your total points, CO2 savings, and recent activities.

What if I have issues redeeming points?

Contact our support team through the Contact page or email us at support@greenliving.com for assistance.
