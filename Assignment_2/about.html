About Green Living Rewards

Our Mission

At Green Living Rewards, our mission is to inspire and incentivize sustainable living practices worldwide. We aim to create a positive environmental impact by rewarding individuals for adopting eco-friendly habits and making conscious choices that benefit our planet.

Our History

Founded in 2020 by a group of environmental enthusiasts, Green Living Rewards started as a small initiative to encourage local communities to reduce their carbon footprint. Since then, we've grown into a global program, partnering with eco-conscious businesses and reaching thousands of users committed to sustainable living.
